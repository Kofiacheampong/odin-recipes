Waakye

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Black-eyed peas
  • Red beans
  • Millet leaves (optional)
  • Garlic, ginger, onions, scotch pepper
  • Bay leaves
  • Thyme
  • Salt

Steps

Steps to Prepare Waakye

  1. Rinse the rice and beans several times with clean water to remove any dirt or debris.
  2. Soak the rice and beans in water for about 30 minutes.
  3. Drain the water and rinse the rice and beans again.
  4. Add the rice and beans to a pot and add enough water to cover them by about an inch.
  5. Add salt, sliced onions, and spices to the pot and stir well.
  6. Cook the rice and beans over medium heat until they are tender and all the water has been absorbed. This should take about 45-50 minutes.
  7. While the rice and beans are cooking, prepare the stew and other accompaniments.
  8. Once the rice and beans are cooked, fluff them with a fork and serve with the stew, spaghetti, fried plantains, and other accompaniments.
