Fried Rice

Easy 10 minute fried rice recipe, with natural flavours and 60 grams of protein.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup of frozen peas and carrots
  • 1/4 cup of diced onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook rice according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add diced onions, garlic, and ginger, and stir-fry until fragrant.
  4. Add diced carrots and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add peas, corn, and diced bell pepper, and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Push the vegetables to the side of the skillet or wok and add beaten eggs.
  7. Scramble the eggs until cooked and mix them with the vegetables.
  8. Add the cooked rice to the skillet or wok and mix well.
  9. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil to the skillet or wok and mix well.
  10. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly, until the rice is heated through and evenly coated with the sauce.
  11. Garnish with sliced green onions and serve hot.
